About Us

Being one of the few Irish vegan-based websites we feel that it is our duty to bring you the very best vegan food has to offer. Most of our recipes are designed for beginners to follow and even if you don’t want to spend your time making our delicious recipes you can just browse our site.

Our Vision

As a small group of 4 developers we decided to branch out as we seen there was a lack of vegan focused websites available. All of our dishes and recipes have been adapted from original recipes from various chefs and sources from all over the world.

Future Plans

In the future we hope to spread the word about veganism and we would like to build a brand for ourselves we know that this is a long way away but we think that with your support we can make a big difference. This website will serve as the foundation for our future so we hope you enjoy it.
